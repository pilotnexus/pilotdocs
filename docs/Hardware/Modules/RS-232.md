---


### Applications

- Measurement systems.
- Debug terminals.
- Simple PC communication.
- PLCs.
- Other RS-232 connected devices.

---


### Possible configurations 

- 2xRS-232 (Rx/Tx).
- RS-232 + Hardware-Handshake.

---


### Speed 

9600, 19200, 38400, 57600, 115200 baud

---


### Dimensions

36.6mm x 29.8mm (1.441in x 1.173in).
